Privacy Policy for Pssst!

Thank you for using Pssst! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

Data Collection and Usage

Pssst! does not collect, store, or share any personal data. However, the App requires access to certain device features to function properly:

  • Notifications Access

    The App accesses your notifications to manage and silence them as per your configuration. No notification content is stored, shared, or sent to any external servers.

  • Background Location Access

    The App uses background location to detect specific Wi-Fi networks for muting purposes. Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not collected, stored, or shared with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Pssst! does not integrate or interact with any third-party services that collect user data.

User Privacy

We respect your privacy. All data processing happens locally on your device, ensuring no personal information is transmitted or stored externally.
